Roles & Responsibilities

Lead developer in charge of designing and implementing the anti-theft system.

Project Details

The Anti-AutoTheft Bike System uses GPS tracking and motion sensors to detect unauthorized movement. The system sends real-time alerts to the owner's mobile device and can be tracked via a dedicated app.

Impacts

This project significantly reduces bike theft incidents by providing a reliable and efficient method to monitor and protect bicycles. The integration of GPS tracking allows for quick recovery of stolen bikes.
