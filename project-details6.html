Roles & Responsibilities

Lead designer responsible for conceptualizing and prototyping the adjustable mount for Apple phones.

Project Details

The Apple Phone Adjustable Mount enhances usability and accessibility by allowing users to position their phones at various angles. The design includes secure locking mechanisms and compatibility with different phone sizes.

Impacts

This project improves the user experience by providing a flexible and stable mounting solution for Apple phones. It is particularly useful for hands-free operations, such as video calls and content viewing.
